### Origin

This account was unearthed during a routine archeological dig of old email inboxes. The specimen dates to a time when the owner was 13, his English was held together by Google Translate, and he thought `SetPlayerHealth(playerid, 99999)` was the pinnacle of game design.

The original coder has since evolved into a less embarrassing version of himself. You can find him at [github.com/lynicis](https://github.com/lynicis), current model, fewer bugs, still writes pawn in his dreams.

### Developer Toolchain

The specimen was recovered from a dual boot setup. Windows 7 for the games, Ubuntu for the part where you tell people you use Linux and then spend the rest of the night trying to get the wifi driver to work.

- **Dev-C++** (blue icon flavor). The compiler crashed more than the code. Debugging was printf or simply crying.
- **Notepad++** with the green dragon. Fancy notepad with tabs. Felt like a professional.
- **Ubuntu** with the purple theme and a broken sudo from yesterday's experiment.
- **Windows 7** with the Aero theme. That watermark saying "this copy of Windows is not genuine" was definitely real, you could just tell.
